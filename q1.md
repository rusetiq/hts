### 1. Convert Sentence to Cow Latin

You are given a sentence `s` consisting of words separated by single spaces. Convert the sentence to **Cow Latin** using the following rules:

1. If a word begins with a **vowel** (`a, e, i, o, u`, case-insensitive), append `"moo"` to the end of the word.  
2. If a word begins with a **consonant**, move the first letter to the end of the word, then append `"moo"`.  
3. Add one letter `"m"` to the end of the word for each word index (starting from `1`).  
4. The words in the final sentence should be separated by single spaces.  
5. There should be no leading or trailing spaces in the final result.

---

### Example 1:

**Input:**  
`s = "I love milk"`  

**Output:**  
`"Imoom lovemoomm ilkmmoomm"`  

**Explanation:**  
- `"I"` → starts with vowel → `"I" + "moo" + "m"` → `"Imoom"`  
- `"love"` → starts with consonant → `"ove" + "l" + "moo" + "mm"` → `"lovemoomm"`  
- `"milk"` → starts with consonant → `"ilk" + "m" + "moo" + "mmm"` → `"ilkmmoomm"`  

---

### Example 2:

**Input:**  
`s = "The quick brown fox"`  

**Output:**  
`"heTmoom uickqmoomm rownbmoommm oxfmoommmm"`

---

### Constraints:

- `1 <= s.length <= 150`
- `s` consists only of English letters and spaces.
- Words are separated by a single space.
- There are no leading or trailing spaces.
