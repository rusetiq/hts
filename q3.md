### 3. Magic Potion Mixing

You are given an array `ingredients` where each element represents the **magical strength** of an ingredient.  
You can mix **any two ingredients** to create a potion. The **power** of a potion is defined as the **product** of the strengths of the two ingredients.  

Return the **maximum power** potion you can create.

---

### Example 1:

**Input:**  
`ingredients = [3, 5, 2, 7]`

**Output:**  
`35`

**Explanation:**  
- Mixing ingredients `5` and `7` gives the maximum power: `5 * 7 = 35`.

---

### Example 2:

**Input:**  
`ingredients = [1, 2, 3, 1]`

**Output:**  
`6`

**Explanation:**  
- Mixing `2` and `3` gives the maximum power: `2 * 3 = 6`.

---

### Constraints:

- `2 <= ingredients.length <= 10^5`  
- `1 <= ingredients[i] <= 10^4`
